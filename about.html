About Thom

Thomas "Thom" Manning is a digital media producer, mainly focusing on short films and video essays. He is currently studying Information Science at Michigan State University. He mainly discusses films, video games, politics, and music, specifically focusing on the consequences of the internet and internet culture on these topics. He has gone by many pseudonyms throughout his time on the internet, including:

  • Thomelette
  • TDT
  • The Captain
  • Mitskifan178
  • xatudrip

Thom mainly creates content using products in the Adobe Creative suite, notably Premiere and Photoshop. He plays a variety of instruments including the saxophone, guitar, and piano. He is an avid Super Smash Bros. competitor, using Sheik in Melee and Pokemon Trainer in Ultimate. He is deeply involved in the esports community at MSU, directing and broadcasting a variety of events. He hopes to compete on Jeopardy one day.
